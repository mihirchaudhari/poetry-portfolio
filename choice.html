Characterization Poem

For so long we have been together

Like a piece of thick leather

Oh, the meories we shared

Will anything compare?


Literally, it hurts that you are off

All this pain, I had to quaff

For you, they just took your pain

I fear our friendship will wane


Where you would be, that place is swollen

But our bond can't be stolen

Pain, but I can still feel you

I'm sure that you miss me, too


"You're broken beyond repair" they say

But that won't get in my way

Because you are my friend, see

And that's all you need to be
