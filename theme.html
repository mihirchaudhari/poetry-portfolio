Shattered Pot

Theme Poem

"Shhh, my mother whispers, "It'll be okay"

But this lie will not fix this accident

Thousands of pieces lay on the parquet

I shake my head, angry that my pot's bent


Too many pieces, how will I fix it

Maybe I can repair it one by one

I'm still in a complete funk, but have grit

Since my hopeless pot is now getting done


With help from friends, the pot is made anew

Although it will never be better than

the original pot that was brand new

I know not what I can't, but what I can
