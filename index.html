Characterization

I’ve reached my finish line

Although they say “You're doing fine”

why me, Why me, WHY ME!!!

My life’s debris, why can’t they see

Hurts to think, hurts to dine


That is when I would whine

The future is going to shine

You’ll see, you’ll see, you’ll see

How much better living can be

I’ll be doing just fine


My leg is frankenstein

But I’m fine above my waistline

Badly, badly, badly

I want to run and become me

But I can’t cross this line


There is so much sunshine

But in my world, I won’t align

Messy, messy, messy

Is me if I walk out blindly

Which is why I still whine


But healing is a sign

That my coming future is all mine

Be free, be free, be free

Of any cages there may be

Make a new starting line

My Vessel

Conflict

My ship has crashed along the rocky shore

The anchor of 20 pounds has sunk in

The engine of my vessel has been tore

The chances of sailing again are thin


There is no way that I can fix this ship

Soon the lost parts are going to go adrift

Or maybe I just need to get a grip

There is a way that my spirits will lift


The impure parts are the ones I will slay

One step at a time I will make it new

My brand new ship will sail away today

Now I know where the ship is going to


Uncharted waters with sights to behold

With old dents a new current will unfold

Shattered Pot

Theme

"Shhh, my mother whispers, "It'll be okay"

But this lie will not fix this accident

Thousands of pieces lay on the parquet

I shake my head, angry that my pot's bent


Too many pieces, how will I fix it

Maybe I can repair it one by one

I'm still in a complete funk, but have grit

Since my hopeless pot is now getting done


With help from friends, the pot is made anew

Although it will never be better than

the original pot that was brand new

I know not what I can't, but what I can
